Hey there, I'm Mark!

Perpetually curious individual.

I currently serve as the Director of Social Good Consulting for the Data Science Society, where I previously held roles as a project manager and consultant.

My experience includes working with renowned non-profit organizations such as Stanford Medicine, World Wildlife Fund and The Nature Conservancy, as well as with Universal Health Services. In these roles, I collaborated with my teams to address diverse data science needs through predictive analytics, large language models (LLMs), mapping, and other data-driven tools and solutions.

Not only do I love making societal, business, and environmental inferences through data, but I also love turning them into cool maps using GIS tools!
